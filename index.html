Biograph

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) under the supervision of Prof. Chao Ma. I have been a researcher at Tencent from 2021 to 2022. Before that, I received the joint M.S. degree with the University and Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), and the Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Prof. Hu Han and Prof. Shiguang Shan in 2021. I also collaborated with Prof. Xilin Chen closely. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, and artificial intelligence.
