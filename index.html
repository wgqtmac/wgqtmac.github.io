Biograph

I received my B.E. degree from Nankai University, Tianjin, China in 2016. I am currently a Ph.D student of Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Shiguang Shan. I also collaborate with Dr. Hu Han closely. During 2019.10-2020.1, I was an intern at University of Oulu under the supervision of Prof. Guoying Zhao. My researches mainly focus on affective computing, including remote heart rate estimation, facial action units detection, gaze estimation, etc. I am also interested in basic theories of machine learning and their applications in computer version.
