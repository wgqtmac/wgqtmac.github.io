Biograph

I am a Ph.D student at Shanghai Jiaotong University under the supervision of Prof. Chao Ma. I have been a researcher at Tencent since 2021. I have received the joint M.S. degree with the Uninversity and Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences under the supervision of Prof. Hu Han in 2021. I also collaborated with Prof. Shiguang Shan and Prof. Xilin Chen closely. My research interests include computer vision, pattern recognition, and image processing. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree from the Department of Computer Science, North China Electric Power University (NCEPU).
